Original Guide By MakuAureo

My objective with this guide is to provide tips/strategies for stamina usage and examples of good stamina management to help people understand a mechanic that I think is very intuitive, but at the same time hard to master if you don’t actively play with it in mind.

🔥 Stamina Basics

  • Stamina depletes only when **running** or **jumping**.
  • Jumping **uses stamina** but **does not increase horizontal speed**.
  • More weight = **slower movement** & **faster stamina depletion**.
  • Stamina **regenerates at the same rate** regardless of weight.
  • Walking **slows down stamina regeneration**.
  • Taking damage **increases stamina** proportional to the amount of damage.

⚡ Basic Stamina Strategies

  • Don’t jump unless necessary.
  • Stand still while waiting for stamina to regenerate (e.g., before hive).
  • Never let your stamina completely fill up.
  • Running is **more efficient with less weight**.
  • Run **down slopes** and walk **up slopes**.

⚠️ Stamina Mistake: Many players think using all stamina in one go is best. However, keeping **reserve stamina** for emergencies (e.g., surprise monsters) is often more beneficial.

💨 Advanced Stamina Usage

To master stamina, you need to understand how acceleration works:

  • Running **does not instantly reach max speed**—it accelerates over time.
  • Acceleration starts slow, peaks, and then stops once you reach top speed.
  • To minimize acceleration time loss, use **single sprint bursts**.
  • Acceleration **only resets after walking or crouching**.

For a visual explanation, see Thalonius' video on **momentum preservation**.

🏠 Inside vs. 🌲 Outside Stamina Management

Inside:

  • While looting, **maintain near-full stamina** before dropping items at a pile.
  • Drop items **while standing still** to minimize movement waste.
  • Distribute weight evenly to avoid **slower, stamina-draining trips**.
  • Use **full stamina** when weightless but keep some reserve for emergencies.
  • Run to a dead end first, then **loot back** toward the exit.

Outside:

  • Use **only the necessary stamina** to reach max acceleration.
  • For **cash registers**, use extra stamina as needed.
  • Near the ship, **keep stamina close to full** for last-second sprints.
  • With **Tzp**, ensure at least **half stamina** before inhaling.
  • Without Tzp, **juggle** items to optimize movement.
